Digital Marketing

Digital marketing is a platform for the marketing of products or services on the Internet. This has various platforms are such SEO, SEM, SMO, ASO, ORM, PPC and so on. Digitial marketing has changed the way that we want to promote our brands widely on Internet.

SEO

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is a long time process to increase visibility of your website by getting high rankings on search engines through organic search results.

SEM

Search Engine Marketing (SEM) is an Internet Marketing which focuses on increasing visibility of a website in search engine result pages.

SMO

Social Media option (SMO) is a process of gaining traffic through various social media sites are such as Facebook, Google+, Twitter and Blog sites to increase the awareness of brands, products or services.

ORM

Online Reputation Management (ORM) is the practice of monitoring the reputation of an individual firm. It is based on with re-building company's brand by tracking either negative or positive responses using SEO tactics.

Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate Marketing is a business marketing strategy planning in a business rewards one or more affiliates for each visitor or customer. It is all about engaging advertiser, promoter, or customer.

Content Marketing

Content Marketing is a marketing technique publising a relevant, valuable and unique contents attract visitors and making them into a potential customer on various format.
